Kashi Raisin Vineyard Cereal

Organic Promise® Raisin Vineyard™ is an organic take on an old favorite with 15g whole grains.

Non-GMO Product Verified and USDA certified organic, this reinvented breakfast classic is packed with crispy rice, wheat, and quinoa flakes and absolutely nothing artificial.

We added a touch of cinnamon and plump, sun-ripened Thompson raisins in every bite to for a delicious blend of flavors, textures, and nutrition. Plus, it’s low in fat and vegan to boot. It’s a breakfast classic--all grown up.
