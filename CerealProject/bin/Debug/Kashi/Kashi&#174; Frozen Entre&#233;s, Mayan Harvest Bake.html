Kashi® Frozen Entreés, Mayan Harvest Bake

Wholesome and tasty, this baked entrée does spice right with plantains, polenta and ancho chili sauce.

Our unique blend of Seven Whole Grains has inspired some tasty creations in our Kashi® All Natural Frozen Entrees.

Plantains, roasted sweet potato, black beans and kale with a spicy ancho sauce served over Kashi® 7 Whole Grains & Sesame™ Pilaf, amaranth and polenta topped with a pumpkin seed garnish.

We take great care in roasting, grilling, and seasoning these ingredients to let their real flavors come through. With vegan and vegetarian options as well, there's an entree for everyone.
