Kashi® Chewy Granola Bars, Cherry Dark Chocolate

Whole grains, dark chocoloate, tart red cherries and roasted nuts make for a bar with real yummy goodness.

Chewy and naturally tasty, our granola bars satisfy your sweet and wholesome sides. Enjoy lightly sweetened red tart cherries, dark chocolate and hearty rolled grains — with the benefit of 4g fiber and 5g protein.

Naturally, you won't find any partially hydrogenated oils, high fructose corn syrup, or artificial anything but what you will find in our chewy granola bars is just what you'd expect from Kashi® –minimally processed, pure ingredients, and natural goodness you can see as well as taste.

- 15g Whole Grains*
- 5g Protein
- 3g Fiber**
*48g or more of whole grains recommended daily.
**2g total fat per serving

Development of heart disease depends upon many factors, but its risk may be reduced by diets low in saturated fat and cholesterol and healthy lifestyles.
