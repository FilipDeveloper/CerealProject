Kashi® Snack Crackers, Country Cheddar

Real cheddar cheese gives our savory whole grain crackers just a bit of kick.

Kashi® Snack Crackers are the lovable snack that loves you back. Cheese lovers, you'll enjoy every savory bite of these crispy snackers.

Country Cheddar cheese crackers are made with real cheddar for a subtle, satisfying tang, making them a go-to munchie with dip or without.

Our Snack Crackers let you snack and feel good about it, because they're made with our Seven Whole Grain blend and without any trans fats — so there's nothing you won't love.

At Kashi, we put our hearts and minds into bringing you naturally great-tasting foods for your health and wellness.
